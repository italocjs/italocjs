##

I'm a software and product engineer specializing in embedded systems and industrial IoT. I work across the full product lifecycle: concept, prototyping, electronics, firmware, backend, deployment, and operations. Based in Brazil, working internationally.

Currently consulting in LATAM, building industrial telemetry and fleet management systems for forestry, mining, and construction.

**Embedded & Hardware**
ESP32, STM32, FreeRTOS, PCB design + fabrication.

**Protocols**
CAN bus (J1939, OBD2, TWAI), MQTT, BLE/BT SPP, LoRa, GPS/GNSS, I2C, SPI, Satellite (Orbcomm, Swarm, Globalstar, Starlink)

**Backend & Infrastructure**
Python, REST APIs, Docker, Proxmox, Linux, VPS, automation pipelines

**Platforms**
Linux, Windows, macOS, Android — cross-platform development and deployment

**Industries**
Forestry, mining, construction, fleet management, manufacturing, medical R&D
